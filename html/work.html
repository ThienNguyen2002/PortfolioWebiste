Sophomore Year

Asian American Student Association: Director of Conference

(August 2021 – May 2022)

As the leader of a 15-member student team, I orchestrated the successful planning, preparation, and coordination of the Asian American High School Conference on behalf of the Asian American Student Association and the University of Utah. Through collaboration with various departments on campus, local communities, venues, facilitators, speakers, performers, and volunteers, I ensured the event's seamless execution. We hosted about 100 students and 10 school advisors from high schools across Utah, offering them a wide range of engaging activities and workshops. As the accountable party for the event's success, I am proud to have delivered a meaningful experience for all involved. I was awarded a $1000 Utah Asian Charitable Foundation Scholarship by the Utah Asian Chamber of Commerce a few months after the event.
